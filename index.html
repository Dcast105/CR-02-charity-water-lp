Real Impact: Meet Amina

Amina is a 12 year old girl from Ethiopia who used to walk miles every day to collect water for her family. Thanks to your support, her village now has a clean water well. Amina can go to school and her family is healthier and happier.

Our Story

Charity: Water is dedicated to solving the global water crisis by bringing clean and safe drinking water to people in need. We believe that lasting change comes from education and engagement—especially among young individuals. By inspiring and empowering the next generation of changemakers, we can build a movement that continues to make an impact for years to come. Join us as we educate youth about the importance of clean water, the challenges faced by millions worldwide, and how everyone can help create a healthier, brighter future for all.
